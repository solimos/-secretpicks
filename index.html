The Secret Pick
"Trust me, this one’s going to explode." You heard it from someone who *knew*. A secret pick. A hidden gem. A coin no one was watching, but it had *potential*. The whispers grew louder, and the price soared. For a moment, it felt like you had cracked the code. But then, the unexpected dip. The panic. Was it really a secret? Or just another trap?
— In the world of "secret picks," the line between success and failure is thin.
